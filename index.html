About B-Beauty

B-Beauty is A cosmetic brand. You can find your own makeup style here, buy the most suitable cosmetics for you, and become a more beautiful yourself. Founded in 2003, B-Beauty Cosmetics Co., Ltd. is a wholly owned American company. With the support of the Central Research Institute in East Lansing and New York, as well as the production base of products, it has become a cosmetics research and development, production, sales and service as one of the enterprises. With East Lansing as the center, the company has set up branches in various regions of the country, creating beauty for women all over the world with high-quality products and professional services.
